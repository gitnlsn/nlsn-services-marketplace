Become Professional Screen

Purpose: Onboarding flow for users transitioning from customer to professional service provider status.

Screen Overview

The Become Professional screen guides users through a comprehensive onboarding process to set up their professional profile, verify credentials, configure payment methods, and start offering services on the platform.

Onboarding Steps

Step 1: Basic Information

Required Fields

  • Full legal name
  • Date of birth
  • CPF/CNPJ (tax ID)
  • Phone number (with WhatsApp option)
  • Professional email
  • Business address

Step 2: Professional Details

Profile Setup

  • Professional headline (60 characters)
  • Professional bio (500 characters)
  • Years of experience
  • Certifications and qualifications
  • Languages spoken
  • Service area radius

Step 3: Service Categories

Category Selection

  • Primary service category
  • Secondary categories (up to 3)
  • Specialized skills within categories
  • Service delivery method (on-site, remote, hybrid)
