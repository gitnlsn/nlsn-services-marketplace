The Vision

Savoir Link is a dynamic ecosystem where professionals can both showcase their skills and discover the services they need. It's a place for creators, artisans, and experts to connect, collaborate, and grow their businesses.

Core Pillars

  • Unified User Model: Every user is both a potential client and a professional. There are no artificial barriers, just a single, flexible account.
  • Rich Service Portfolios: Go beyond simple listings. Users can create beautiful, multi-page showcases for each of their services, complete with photo galleries.
  • AI-Powered Discovery: Semantic search powered by embeddings to understand the intent behind searches, not just keywords.
  • Secure Payment Processing: Pagarme-powered escrow system with 15-day holding period protects both clients and professionals.
  • Multi-Channel Notifications: Keep users informed with timely alerts via SMS, WhatsApp, and email, powered by Twilio.
  • Direct Communication: Professionals can display contact information publicly and communicate directly with clients.
