Welcome to the Marketplace Documentation

This documentation provides a comprehensive overview of the architecture, features, and flows of the peer-to-peer service marketplace.

The Vision

Our platform is a dynamic ecosystem where professionals can both showcase their skills and discover the services they need. It's a place for creators, artisans, and experts to connect, collaborate, and grow their businesses.

Core Pillars

  • Unified User Model: Every user is both a potential client and a professional. There are no artificial barriers, just a single, flexible account.
  • Rich Service Portfolios: Go beyond simple listings. Users can create beautiful, multi-page showcases for each of their services, complete with photo galleries.
  • Intuitive Discovery: We use cutting-edge semantic search to understand the intent behind a search, not just the keywords. This leads to more accurate and relevant matches.
  • Secure and Transparent Payments: Our Pagarme-powered escrow system protects both clients and professionals, ensuring a fair and trustworthy transaction process.
  • Multi-Channel Notifications: We keep users informed with timely alerts via SMS, WhatsApp, and email, powered by Twilio.
  • Direct Communication: Professionals can choose to display their contact information publicly, allowing for direct communication with potential clients.
