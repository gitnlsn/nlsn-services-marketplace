How It Works

Savoir Link is a marketplace that connects service providers with customers. Simply search for the service you need, compare providers, and book directly through our secure platform.

How do I sign up as a service provider?

Simply click "Get Started" and choose "Offer Services" during registration. You'll need to verify your identity and provide details about your skills and experience.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods including PayPal and Apple Pay. All transactions are processed securely.

How are service providers vetted?

All service providers undergo background checks, identity verification, and skill assessment. We also maintain a review system to ensure ongoing quality.

What if I'm not satisfied with a service?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with a service, contact our support team within 48 hours and we'll work to resolve the issue or provide a refund.

Are there any fees for using the platform?

For customers, browsing and booking services is free. Service providers pay a small commission on completed bookings to help us maintain the platform.

About Savoir Link

Savoir Link connects skilled professionals with customers who need their services. Our platform makes it easy to find, book, and pay for quality services in your area.
