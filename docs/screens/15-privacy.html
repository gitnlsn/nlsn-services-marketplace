1. Introduction

Welcome to Savoir Link! This Privacy Policy explains how Savoir Link, Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Your Privacy Matters:

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. We are committed to collecting only the information necessary to provide our services and keeping it secure.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using our Service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, and profile photo
  • Profile Information: Bio, location, skills, certifications, and portfolio content
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, bank account information, billing address
  • Communication: Messages, reviews, ratings, and support inquiries
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, professional licenses, insurance documents

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information:

Information Type Examples Purpose
Device Information IP address, device type, operating system, browser type Security, troubleshooting, analytics
Usage Data Pages visited, time spent, clicks, search queries Service improvement, personalization
Location Data GPS coordinates, city/region (with permission) Service matching, local search
Cookies & Similar Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies Authentication, preferences, analytics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third-party sources:

  • Social media platforms (when you connect your accounts)
  • Background check and verification services
  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Marketing partners and referral sources

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate connections between customers and service providers
  • Process bookings, payments, and transactions
  • Enable messaging and communication features
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes

3.2 Safety and Security

  • Verify user identities and professional credentials
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Comply with legal requirements and law enforcement requests

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations

3.4 Communication

  • Send important service notifications and updates
  • Provide marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Conduct surveys and request feedback
  • Send promotional offers and recommendations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users

To facilitate our marketplace, we share certain information with other users:

  • Public profile information (name, photo, bio, ratings)
  • Service listings and availability
  • Communication necessary for bookings and service delivery
  • Reviews and ratings (anonymized when appropriate)

4.2 With Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Identity verification services
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Customer support platforms
  • Analytics and marketing tools

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights:

  • In response to legal process or government requests
  • To investigate and prevent fraud or security issues
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of users
  • In connection with business transfers or acquisitions
Important:

We will notify you of any significant changes to how we share your information, unless prohibited by law.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure hosting infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations
  • Regular security monitoring and threat detection
  • Access controls and role-based permissions

5.2 Operational Safeguards

  • Employee training on data privacy and security
  • Background checks for employees with data access
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Compliance with industry security standards

5.3 Your Role in Security

While we implement strong security measures, you also play a role in keeping your information secure:

  • Use a strong, unique password for your account
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Keep your device and browser software updated
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

Data Type Retention Period Purpose
Account Information Until account deletion + 30 days Service provision, account recovery
Transaction Records 7 years after transaction Legal compliance, tax records
Communication Data 3 years after last message Support, dispute resolution
Analytics Data 26 months maximum Service improvement, analytics
Security Logs 12 months maximum Security monitoring, fraud prevention

When we no longer need your information, we securely delete or anonymize it. Some information may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information. The availability of these rights may depend on your location and applicable laws:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Export: Download your account data from your settings page

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Profile Updates: Edit your profile information in your account settings
  • Correction Requests: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Verification Updates: Update your identity verification documents

7.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Selective Deletion: Request deletion of specific information
  • Processing Restriction: Limit how we use your information

7.4 Communication Preferences

  • Email Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing emails
  • Notification Settings: Control push notifications and alerts
  • Communication Channels: Choose how we contact you
How to Exercise Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@savoirlink.com or use the privacy controls in your account settings.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform:

8.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze site usage and improve performance
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and personalize your experience
  • Targeting Cookies: Used for advertising and marketing (with your consent)

8.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Our cookie preference center (when available)
  • Opt-out tools provided by advertising networks
  • Privacy-focused browser extensions

8.3 Third-Party Tracking

We work with third-party services that may track your activity:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media pixels for advertising
  • Customer support chat widgets
  • Payment processor tracking

9. International Transfers

Savoir Link operates globally, and your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for these transfers:

9.1 Transfer Mechanisms

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions for transfers to certain countries
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct
  • Explicit consent where required by law

9.2 Data Processing Locations

Your data may be processed in:

  • United States (primary data centers)
  • European Union (for EU users)
  • Other countries where our service providers operate

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you via email for material changes
  • Post a prominent notice on our website
  • Provide in-app notifications when appropriate

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
